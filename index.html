UNILEVER FMCG MEAL AUTOMATION

a gap analyses of employee workflow reveals that too much worktime was spent after break time trying to get meals from Company's Canteen. After gathering requirements and writing necessary business documents, the canteen process was automated into a webpage which saved Unilever money and improved employee satisfaction.

EXCEL SUPERSTORE

Using Power BI I built a dashboard that summarizes all the monthly Sales, Profit and other vital information of the superstore in a dashboard. Subsequently the system generates same charts per year as more data is added. This helps the superstore improve their profit and sales all accross their branches in United States.

Travel Company Project

a travel company seeking to understand the needs of its customers in order to provide services tailored to customer specific requirements. I gathered customer's requirements using a survey that asked six important questions. Then, i Used Microsoft Excel to analyze my responses which the company used to offer tailored promotional services that boosted their sales turn over.

XpresTech Food Delivery

An online Food delivery Company wanted insights on not just the delivery part of the company but also customer feedback about the meals. By Using Power BI for analyses and visualization, I provide the needed insights which was used to impliment a new/easier payment option. This increased customer usage of their app.

BSMC High school Reunion

An association of old high school students wanted to determine if their 20 years anniversary party should hold and what should be planned and provided during the party. I designed a google form survey that was used to collect their opinions into an excel sheet, analyzed it and presented the result in a Power point slide that was used to plan the anniversary.

Malaria Deaths in Nigeria

A Nigeria ministry in the health sector wanted to know the trend of malaria deaths in Nigeria. From the world statistics online (wikipedia) that showed Total deaths within the year needed for all countries of the world, I used Microsoft Excel Power query to clean the data and power pivot to extract needed part.
